## To address challenges like this, Berlynsea and I built closed-loop orchestration layers comprised of AI Agents that allows operations teams to quickly analyze the financial impact of a breakdown, identify the best rerouting options, and coordinate updates across logistics partners and retail clients in real time.

![](https://github.com/joesghub/building_pepsico_agentic_landscape/blob/main/slides/5.png?raw=true)

### The result? Resolution processes that are faster, smarter, and more cost-efficient. This helps everyone involved and PepsiCo can keep creating more smiles with every sip and every bite!
